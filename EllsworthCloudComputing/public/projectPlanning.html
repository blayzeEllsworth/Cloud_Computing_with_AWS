Transitioning to the Cloud

  • Consider which cloud provider fits your business the best: AWS, Google Cloud, or Microsoft Azure.
  • Consider each information system in your business that is generating meaningful data, then transfer the data to the cloud with encryption. Test the data after ETL is completed, to ensure no data was corrupted or lost.
  • Determine if your existing business applications are compatible with the Cloud. If they are not consider upgrading to a cloud-optimized platform.
  • Migrate applications and optimize the cloud infrastructure to enhance your website's efficiency.
  • Before launching, be sure to test each application to verify it meets functional and non-functional requirements.
  • Train company personnel on how to use and navigate the Cloud and use monitoring tools to track your company's website performance and user experience.
